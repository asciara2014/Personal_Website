ANTHONY SCIARA JR.

an electrical engineering student who has a passion for innovative technology, digital content, and the great outdoors.

Welcome to my website. I am a electrical engineering student, digital content creator, senior at Texas A&M University - Texarkana (TAMUT), and IEEE chair at TAMUT. In the past few months I have worked on several projects alongside my formal education. My goal is to develop innovative solutions utilizing both my hardware and software experience while continually progressing toward something greater. I have knowledge in Java, C, Python, circuit design, system design, project management, electrical maintenance practices, CAD design, drafting, and robotics. Additionally, I am an avid outdoors-man and enjoy every minute spent in the great outdoor with friends and family. I created this site to share my interest, as they grow and change, and connect with like minded individuals.
